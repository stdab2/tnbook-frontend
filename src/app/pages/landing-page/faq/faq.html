Do I need WhatsApp Business to use this?

Yes, you'll need WhatsApp Business API access. We provide step-by-step guidance to set this up, and for most users, it's a simple upgrade from regular WhatsApp Business (which is free).

How does calendar integration work?

We sync with Google Calendar, Outlook, Apple Calendar, and even Calendly. When someone books through WhatsApp, it automatically blocks the time in your calendar and prevents double-bookings.

Can I customize the booking messages?

Yes! You can create custom message templates, add your branding, and personalize the booking flow to match your communication style and business needs.
